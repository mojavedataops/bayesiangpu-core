Eight Schools Hierarchical Model

Model

The Eight Schools problem (Rubin, 1981):
Eight schools each implemented a coaching program. The observed treatment
effects (y_j) and their standard errors (se_j) are given.

Hierarchical model:
  y_j ~ Normal(alpha_j, sigma_j)      [known sigma_j = se_j]
  alpha_j ~ Normal(mu, tau)            [school-level effects]
  mu ~ Normal(0, 10)                   [population mean]
  tau ~ HalfNormal(5)                  [between-school std dev]

This uses run_hierarchical_model() with an intercept-only specification.
